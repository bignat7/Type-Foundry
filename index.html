Glacier








Glacier is a 7-bit pixel font that interpolates between sharp peaks and caving valleys. It features a truncated stem on one extreme and a taller, extended stem on the other extreme. It intepolates between a display pixel font and a harsh, perilous environment.

ALTITUDE










Altitude is an all-caps pictogram font that captures the thrill of velocity and flight. Made for skyward designers, whimsical wayfarers, and creative jetsetters. Inspired by aircraft aerodynamics, Altitude is crafted in reference to aircraft modules.
It includes letters with laminar as well as turbulent flow.

SLEET










Sleet is an all-caps pixel font that embodies the dispersion of frozen rain. This typeface can be used as a legible but low-fidelity display font, or as a graphic element that becomes more like rain than a letter.
